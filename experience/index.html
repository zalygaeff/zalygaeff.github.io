Softserve

I design a platform that covers life insurance sales lifecycle and provides insurance agencies & carriers with the control and the data on all their activities under one ecosystem (starting from lead nurturing and underwriting to product selection and comparison and presentation). In addition, I am responsible for developing a design system of reusable design patterns.

2018..

Exo

A side-project dedicated to the exploration of the exocortex concept. Essentially, an attempt to decrease the cognitive load related to information chaos caused by the usage of a wide range of tools for planning, scoping and managing the time. The application is heavily inspired by Org-mode and takes the best out of it: outlining, fast navigation and focus on the text as the simplest way to organize the data.

I am involved as a lead designer and partially as a product manager, defining the strategy besides.
